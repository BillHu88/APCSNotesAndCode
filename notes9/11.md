# Logical
`&&` - and, `cond1&&cond2`

`||` - or, `cond1||cond2`

`!` - not,  `!cond1`

# DeMorgan's Law
`!(p&&q)` is the same as `!p||!q`, `not(fun and games)` == not fun or not games


`!(p||q)` is the same as `!p&&!q`

### DeMorgan's Law Practice
